Shipping Policy

Shipping Fees

Orders above ₹2,300 Free Shipping
Orders below ₹2,300 ₹80 flat fee

Dispatch

Orders placed before 2:00 PM on working days are prepared for dispatch on the same day.

Orders placed after 2:00 PM, on weekends, or public holidays will be dispatched the next working day.

Delivery Time

Delivery typically takes 3–5 working days after dispatch, depending on your location. Remote or hilly areas (J&K, North East, Andaman & Nicobar, etc.) may take up to 7–10 working days.

Courier Partner

We ship via Trackon Couriers. You can track your shipment on the Trackon website using the tracking number sent to your email.

Tracking

Once dispatched, your tracking number is sent to the email address provided at checkout. You can also track your order on our Order Tracking page.

Split Shipments

We carry two product lines that ship from separate warehouses:

  • Metal items (trays, decorative objects) — dispatched from our metal warehouse
  • Textile items (pouches, cushions, bags) — dispatched from our textile warehouse

If your order includes both product types, you will receive two separate packages with two tracking numbers. Arrival dates may differ by 1–2 days — this is completely normal and expected.

⚠️ Occasionally items may take slightly longer due to stock availability or supplier delays. We will contact you if your order is affected.
