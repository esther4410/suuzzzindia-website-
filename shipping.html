Shipping Policy

Dispatch

Orders placed before 2:00 PM on working days are prepared for dispatch on the same day.

Orders placed after 2:00 PM, on weekends, or public holidays will be dispatched the next working day.

Delivery Time

Delivery typically takes 3–5 working days after dispatch, depending on your location and courier partner.

Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, your tracking number will be sent to the email or phone number provided at checkout.

⚠️ Some items may take slightly longer due to stock availability or supplier delays. We will notify you if your order is affected.
